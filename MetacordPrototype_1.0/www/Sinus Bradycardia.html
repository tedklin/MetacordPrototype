The heart has eletrical impulses coming from the sinus nodes that create control your heart rate. These electrical impulses travel across the atria and allows the heart to pump blood. Bradycardia is when the electrical signals slow down or become blocked. Heart tissue damage, high blood pressure, congenital heart problem, infections in the heart tissue, heart surgery complications, underactive thyriod gland, deficientcy of the nutrients needed to create the impulses, sleep apnea, inflamatory diseases, build up of iron, and some high blood pressure medicines.
